This is a program, which can help to coordinate colors in quilting.
You can use it for

  • to find new pattern
  • for inspiration
  • to play with colors
You can create your own pattern by clicking triangles on squared canvas.
Or check our swatches by clicking button Next

And try to repeat by clicking the Repeat button
